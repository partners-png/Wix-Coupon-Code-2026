## Wix Hidden Costs to Know Before Buying

Most Wix pricing guides skip these — here's what can add to your bill:

- **Domain renewal** — Free for the first year with any annual paid plan. Renews at approximately $15–$25/year after that depending on extension.
- **App marketplace** — Over 800 third-party apps available, but many premium apps cost $5–$50/month extra. Essential apps for email marketing, forms, and CRM often require paid upgrades.
- **Transaction fees** — Wix charges 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction on most payment processing. No additional platform fee on top.
- **Email marketing** — Free plan includes 200 emails/month. Beyond that, Wix Email Marketing requires a separate paid plan.
- **Multi-year discounts** — Committing to 2–3 years upfront can save up to 50% vs monthly billing. Factor this in if you're planning long-term.
